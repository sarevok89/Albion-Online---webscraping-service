How to use Albion killboard webscraper?

1. Create an account!

Click on "register" button in upper right corner. Type a username, e-mail address and password and click "Sign up" button

2. Login and go to "Webscraper" tab

As You can see all You need to provide is "Fight name" e.g. "PoE vs Arch - Cleyhill", which is gonna be used to name your output file. Your file's name will be today's date followed by "-" and fight name.

3. Get actual killboard links!

Go to https://albiononline.com/en/killboard find the kill You need, click on "details", copy it's url and paste it below.

You can even copy them straight from your guild's Discord.

4. Press "Submit" and...

Now all You need to do is give it some time. Now the script needs to go to each of this link, fetch the data and put it in an Excel file. It usually takes from few seconds, up to even a dozen of minutes. It all depends on how many links You provided.

5. Where's my file?

Once the file is ready it will appear appear in your "Files" page. Simply click the "Download" to get it.

6. That's all!

Now You can easily compensate your guildmates, who had fallen in that fight.
